Our Mission

Since 1916, Goodwill Southern California’s mission has been to Transform Lives Through the Power of Work. We serve individuals with disabilities and disadvantages by providing education, training, work experience and job placement services.

Environmental Promise

Reduce, Reuse and Recycle is what we do. We take pride in our role as an environmentally responsible organization. For example, each year we receive more than 2.6 million donations of unwanted items. Reselling these goods not only helps fund training and job placement for over 32,000 people, it also saves over 100 million pounds of usable goods and waste from landfills.

Cornerstones

For nearly a century, Goodwill Southern California has transformed the lives of individuals with disabilities and disadvantages, providing them greater independence, self-sufficiency, dignity and purpose.

Environmental Promise

We take pride in our role as an environmentally responsible organization. For example, each year we receive more than 2.6 million donations of unwanted items. Reselling these goods not only helps fund training and job placement for over 32,000 people, it also saves over 100 million pounds of usable goods and waste from landfills.

Strict environmental policies guide all of our business practices and those of our vendor partners. Goodwill Southern California’s green efforts have this wonderful, additional side effect: Improving the health and well-being of the global community.
