Do not be lazy! Work with Us!

As one of the leading non-profit brands in the world, and the largest non-profit in Southern California, we have been transforming lives through the power of work for over 100 years. Goodwill Southern California operates a vast network of retail stores, donation centers, career resource centers, work programs for individuals with disabilities, business services, youth programs, and more. We offer a wide variety of full and part-time positions with competitive wages and comprehensive benefits for eligible employees. When you work for Goodwill, you become part of the solution that empowers individuals, advances businesses, enriches the community and cares for the Earth. And we think that feels pretty darn GOOD!
